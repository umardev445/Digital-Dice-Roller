Privacy Policy

Digital Dice Roller - LUXE DICE

📅 Last Updated: June 5, 2026

📋 Welcome to LUXE DICE

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how the LUXE DICE Digital Dice Roller application ("we", "our", or "the app") handles your information. We believe in complete transparency and minimal data collection.

✨ Key Privacy Promise: LUXE DICE is designed to respect your privacy. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. All your dice rolls and game data remain exclusively on your device.

📊 Information We Collect

We collect ZERO personal information. Our app operates entirely on your local device.

What we DON'T collect:

  • ❌ No names, emails, or phone numbers
  • ❌ No location data or IP addresses
  • ❌ No browsing history or usage tracking
  • ❌ No device information or identifiers
  • ❌ No cookies or tracking pixels
  • ❌ No analytics or crash reporting

Local Storage Only (Your Device):

  • ✅ Roll history (last 15 rolls)
  • ✅ Dice statistics (totals, averages, highest roll)
  • ✅ Your selected dice preferences (D6, D20, etc.)

Important: All this data stays on YOUR device using your browser's localStorage. We never have access to this information, and it is automatically removed if you clear your browser data or uninstall the app.

🎯 How Your Data Is Used

Since we don't collect any personal data, there's no usage to describe. The local data stored on your device is used only for:

  • Displaying your roll history within the app
  • Calculating your personal statistics (average, highest roll, etc.)
  • Remembering your preferred dice type between sessions
  • Providing a personalized dice rolling experience

This data never leaves your browser, is never transmitted over the internet, and is never shared with any third party.

🔗 Third-Party Services

LUXE DICE uses NO third-party services. This means:

  • ✅ No Google Analytics or tracking scripts
  • ✅ No advertising networks or ad services
  • ✅ No social media integrations or share buttons that track you
  • ✅ No external APIs that collect data
  • ✅ No CDNs that log your IP address

Everything runs locally in your browser. The only external resource used is your system fonts (Inter font), which is standard for all websites.

🔐 Data Security

Even though we don't collect your data, we still prioritize security:

  • 🔒 All local data is stored in your browser's secure localStorage
  • 🔒 No external data transmission means no risk of data breaches
  • 🔒 Your data remains 100% under your control
  • 🔒 You can clear all data instantly using the "Clear History" button
  • 🔒 Closing your browser tab removes temporary data

💡 You're in control: You can delete all your dice data at any time by clicking the "Clear All" button in the History section or by clearing your browser's local storage.

👶 Children's Privacy

LUXE DICE is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13. Since we collect absolutely no personal information, there are no concerns regarding children's privacy. The app contains no chat features, no user accounts, no messaging, and no way for anyone to contact or identify users.

Parents can feel confident letting their children use LUXE DICE for entertainment, decision-making, or learning about probability.

⚖️ Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location (including GDPR for EU residents and CCPA for California residents), you have the following rights:

  • ✅ Right to Know: You know exactly what data we collect (nothing!)
  • ✅ Right to Delete: You can delete all local data via the Clear History button
  • ✅ Right to Opt-Out: No data collection means nothing to opt out of
  • ✅ Right to Portability: Your data stays on your device

Since we don't collect any personal data, there's no need to submit formal requests. You have complete control over your local data.

📝 Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Since we don't have your contact information, we cannot notify you directly of changes. However, we never change our core commitment: we do not collect your personal data.
